Frequently Asked Questions

General

Where are your products made?

All Rib Rack products are made in the U.S.

Where can I buy Rib Rack products?

You can see a list of our major retailers on the Find Us page of this website. If you need additional details, please go to our contact page and send us a message

Can I buy your products online?

Yes! While we don't sell directly to consumers through our website, you can buy Rib Rack products from our Amazon store.

Why do I have to shake your BBQ sauces and Marinades before I use them?

We do not use artificial thickeners and stabilizers in our products. Therefore, ingredients may separate and settle when our bottles are at rest. For best results, shake before enjoying.

Is it okay to consume Rib Rack products after the “Best By” date?

Our products are freshest and taste best if consumed by the "Best By" date. These dates can be found as follows:
‍
BBQ Sauces: On the neck of the bottle between the neck wrapper and label
Spice Rubs: On the top of the cap
Marinades: On the neck of the bottle between the neck wrapper and label
Pork Rinds: On the back right panel of the bag

Is your packaging recyclable?

All of our bottles - both glass and plastic - are recyclable. Just be sure to rinse them well before recycling.

Nutrition, Allergen and Ingredients

Do your products contain high fructose corn syrup?


No, none of our products contain high fructose corn syrup.

Do your products contain any of the FDA's Top 8 allergens (milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, treenuts, peanuts, wheat, soy)?

We are required by the FDA to disclose the presence of any of the Top 8 allergens on our labels directly below the ingredient statement. Some of our products (i.e. Marinades) contain Soy and this allergen is indicated both on the label and on the Product Detail page of this website.

Please always check the label before consuming Rib Rack products if you have allergy sensitivities.

Are your products Gluten Free?

Yes, all Rib Rack products are gluten free.

Are your products Non GMO?

We work closely with the Non GMO Project to certify all of our products according to their guidelines.

This is a work-in-progress on some Rib Rack items. If you are wondering about a specific product and its non-GMO status, please reference the information on the particular Product Detail page on this website.

You may also check the back of the product label for more information.

How are your Pork Rinds cooked?

Our Pork Rinds are cooked in their own pork fat. We do not use oils/seed oils.

Are your Sugar Free products Keto friendly?

Yes, our Sugar Free product line which includes 3 BBQ Sauces, 3 Spices Rubs and 3 Marinades has been developed to conform to the generally accepted Keto friendly standard for each product category.

What is Allulose?

Allulose is an all-natural sweetener that we use in our Sugar Free products.

It is low calorie but has the same clean, sweet taste you expect from sugar. It exists in nature in very small quantities and can be found in certain fruits including jackfruit, figs and raisins.
